## The Pitch

Stasis provides a durable layer of intelligence between executive intuition and operational reality. By training a proprietary Large Action Model on forty years of internal meeting transcripts and archival memos, the platform identifies the precise delta between what leadership says and what the organization is capable of executing. It removes the friction of human optimism from the strategic planning cycle.

Our flagship integration allows the platform to sit passively in every internal digital communication channel, distilling thousands of hours of discourse into a single, immutable Confidence Score. When the score drops, Stasis automatically freezes discretionary spending and locks calendar availability to prevent further strategic drift. It is the first autonomous governance system designed to protect a company from its own momentum.
