## The Pitch

Basin reconceptualizes the most fundamental utility in the home through a proprietary stack of ultrasonic sensors and cloud-integrated valves. By monitoring flow rates at the molecular level, Basin creates a predictive map of a household’s hydration, hygiene, and irrigation needs, ensuring that water is never merely delivered, but curated.

The hardware suite consists of the Core hub and several Node perimeter sensors that install directly onto existing copper or PEX piping. Through the Basin app, users receive haptic notifications regarding usage anomalies and can adjust the mineral composition of their shower water or the temperature of their kitchen tap with millidegree precision from anywhere in the world.
