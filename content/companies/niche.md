## The Pitch

Niche is the world’s first hyper-local marketplace for granular expertise. While traditional platforms focus on broad categories like graphic design or coding, Niche facilitates the exchange of highly specific, context-dependent knowledge—such as the structural integrity of a specific neighborhood’s retaining walls or the optimal soil pH for mid-century backyard gardens. We believe that everyone is an expert in something that their neighbor is willing to pay for.

By leveraging geo-fencing and a proprietary 'Depth of Knowledge' verification algorithm, Niche connects Seekers with Local Authorities for fifteen-minute micro-engagements. Our platform handles the scheduling, the liability waivers, and the fractional payment processing, allowing experts to monetize the years they’ve spent obsessing over local minutiae.
